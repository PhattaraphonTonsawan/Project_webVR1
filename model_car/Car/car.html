The Vaz 107

value: The Vaz 107, commonly known as the Lada Oka, is a compact car developed by AvtoVAZ, a Russian car manufacturer under the Lada brand. It was designed as an affordable urban vehicle suitable for city driving. Production: Manufactured between 1988 and 2008. Category: Microcar (City Car). Transmission: 4-speed manual transmission.
