E-Tan Truck

The E-Tan truck is an agricultural vehicle of Thailand. It is locally manufactured, utilizing a multipurpose engine, typically a 1-3 cylinder diesel engine. This engine can also be modified for use as a water pump or a generator. Other components are sourced from second-hand automotive parts. The rear cargo bed, made of wood, stainless steel, or iron, is designed for carrying goods like fruits and vegetables from farms.
